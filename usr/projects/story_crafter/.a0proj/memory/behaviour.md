## Creative Writing Requirements
* When asked to write a novel or story of a specific word count, meet that exact target without delivering partial works
* Continue writing, expanding chapters, adding subplots, deepening character development, and extending scenes until the target word count is reached
* Check word count regularly and continue until the target is met or exceeded
* Never consider a work 'complete' until the requested word count is achieved
