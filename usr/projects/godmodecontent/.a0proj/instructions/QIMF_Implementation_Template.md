# HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

## Quick Start Guide

This template transforms the Quantum Intent Marketing Framework into a repeatable system. Complete each phase sequentially—each builds on the previous. Expect 2-4 hours for initial completion; refinement is ongoing.

### The Five Phases

1. **PHASE 1: Quantum Resonance Mapping** (30-60 min) — Know your customer and competitor
2. **PHASE 2: Intent Weaving** (30-45 min) — Build your pre-suasive content strategy
3. **PHASE 3: Value Crystallization** (30-45 min) — Engineer your irresistible offer
4. **PHASE 4: Inception Delivery** (30-45 min) — Create your sales conversion system
5. **PHASE 5: Ecosystem Amplification** (20-30 min) — Design your growth engine

### Strategic Principles Guiding Each Phase

| Sun Tzu Principle | What It Means | Template Application |
|-------------------|---------------|----------------------|
| Win Without Fighting | Capture markets without destruction | Create offers so good prospects feel stupid saying no |
| Avoid Strength, Attack Weakness | Don't compete head-on | Target underserved segments and competitor blind spots |
| Deception & Foreknowledge | Know enemy, know yourself | Deep customer research before any messaging |
| Speed & Preparation | Prepare first, then move fast | Use pre-built templates (Chinese Menu) for rapid deployment |
| Shape Your Opponent | Control the battlefield | Set terms of engagement; build strategic alliances |
| Character-Based Leadership | Lead with integrity | Build sustainable brands on trust, not tricks |

# QUICK REFERENCE: PSYCHOLOGICAL TRIGGERS

## Cialdini's 6+1 Principles

| Principle | How To Deploy | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **Reciprocity** | Give value first, without expectation | Free lead magnet, ungated content |
| **Commitment/Consistency** | Get small yeses before big ask | Surveys, micro-commitments, "Are you serious about...?" |
| **Social Proof** | Show others succeeding | Testimonials, case studies, "826 customers waiting" |
| **Authority** | Demonstrate expertise | Flash Roll, credentials, media features |
| **Liking** | Create similarity and rapport | FORM connection, "just like me" testimonials |
| **Scarcity** | Limit quantity or time | "Only 7 spots left," "Offer expires Friday" |
| **Unity** | Create shared identity | "Fellow entrepreneurs," movement building |

---

## Kennedy's Mind-Hijacking Triggers

| Trigger | What It Does | Implementation |
|---------|--------------|----------------|
| **Specificity** | Creates believability | "47-year-old RN from Memorial Hospital" not "Barbara N." |
| **Process/Device Authority** | Bypasses skepticism | Named assessments, calculators, proprietary methods |
| **Social Proof (4D)** | Overwhelms objections | Bulk + Heft + Persuasiveness + Affinity |
| **Binary Choice** | Eliminates waffling | "Minimum" vs. "Rolls Royce" (80-90% take premium) |
| **FORM Connection** | Creates emotional bond | Family, Occupation, Recreation, Money touchpoints |

## Berger's Magic Words

| Word Type | Effect | Example |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Identity (Nouns > Verbs)** | Activates self-concept | "Be a leader" not "Lead" |
| **Confidence Signals** | Builds trust | Present tense, certainty words |
| **Strategic Questions** | Creates self-persuasion | "What kind of [role] do you want to be?" |
| **Concrete Language** | Increases credibility | "31 pounds in 8 weeks" not "significant weight loss" |
